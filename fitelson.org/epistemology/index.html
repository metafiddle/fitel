Also, I have posted a neat paper by Tomoji Shogenji, which argues that "degree of justification" is not (merely) probability, but is also sensitive to probabilistic relevance. That opens the door for the sort of amendment to Fumerton's Principle of Inferential Justification (†), which I discussed in class today.
