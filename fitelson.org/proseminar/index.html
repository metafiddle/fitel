Proseminar (Fall 2012 | Rutgers | Philosophy)

Time/Location:

Instructors:

Branden Fitelson
Susanna Schellenberg

Description:

Philosophy proseminar, geared toward ﬁrst-year graduate students in philosophy at Rutgers. We will study some highlights of 20th century work in analytic philosophy (focusing mainly on language, epistemology, and mind).

Requirements:

  • 1 question prepared for each class
  • 1 in-class presentation (~20 minutes) with handout (2–4 pages)
  • 1 final paper (15-20 pages) on any of the readings (due 1/6/13)
