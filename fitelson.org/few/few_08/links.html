We now have the contracts at hand for the three hotels at which we have reserved blocks of rooms.   • J. F. Friedrick Inn and Conference Center, 608-231-1341, 866-301-1753: $81 per night (including tax) reserved until April 14, 2008 (mention: Formal Epistemology Workshop). This is a short walk from the workshop venue.

  • Best Western InnTowner and the Highland Club, 608-233-8778, 800-258-8321: $119 per night (plus 14.5% tax) reserved until March 14, 2008 (mention Group Code: PHILO). This is about a mile from campus and has a free (gratuity-based) shuttle that operates by appointment.

  • Clarion Suites Central, 608-284-1234: $109.95 per night (plus 14.5% tax) for a studio, and $129.95 per night (plus 14.5% tax) for a one-bedroom suite including full kitchen, reserved until April 14, 2008 (mention Group Name: UW PHILOSOPHY CONFERENCE). This is about 2 1/2 miles from campus and has no shuttle.

For graduate students: a block of 15 dormitory rooms has been reserved at $35 per night, or $24 per person per night if two people share a room (Short Course Dorms, Humphrey & Jorns Halls, http://www.cals.wisc.edu/students/housing/index.html). These will be held right until the dates of the workshop, and more rooms are available if the need arises. To reserve a room, email dorms@cals.wisc.edu or call 608-262-2270 and press 0 when voice mail begins.
